Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

This is the best peanut butter and jelly sandwhich.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut butter (creamy)
  • Jelly (strawberry)
  • Soft bread

Steps:

  • Use 2 slices of bread
  • Add the peanut butter to one slice of bread
  • Add the jelly to the other slide of bread
  • Put the slices of bread together
  • Enjoy!
