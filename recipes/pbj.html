Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

Description

This is the best peanut butter and jelly sandwhich.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut butter (creamy)
  • Jelly (strawberry)
  • Soft bread

Steps:

  1. Use 2 slices of bread
  2. Add the peanut butter to one slice of bread
  3. Add the jelly to the other slide of bread
  4. Put the slices of bread together
  5. Enjoy!
