Beanie Weenie

Description

Beanie and weenie is a combination of beans and hotdogs, and the sauce is a bbq mix.

Ingredients:

  • BBQ beans
  • Hot dogs (4+)

Steps:

  1. Cut the hot dogs into 1/4 inch pieces
  2. Brown the hot dog slices in a pot
  3. Add the beans and sauce
  4. Cook until desired temp
